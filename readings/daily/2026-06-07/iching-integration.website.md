**Delight (#16)**
thunder, earth, the arousing — these are the elements at play. 
the image of delight is one of thunder coming resounding out of the earth — a powerful, primal sound that shakes the ground beneath your feet. 
this image is an instruction to tap into your own inner source of power and enthusiasm, to let it resonate deeply within you, and to allow it to guide your actions. 
"*enthuisam. it furthers one to install helpers and to set armies marching*" — this is the judgment, and it's saying that when you're in a state of delight, you're more likely to gather people and resources around you, to build momentum and make progress towards your goals. 
but what does it mean to be in a state of delight, really? 
it means being fully engaged, fully alive, and fully present in the moment — it's a state of being that's hard to put into words, but it's one that you know when you feel it. 
delight is a hexagram that names a time of great energy and potential, a time when you have the power to bring people and things together, to create something new and exciting. 
it demands a posture of openness, of receptivity, of being willing to take risks and try new things — it's a time to be bold, to be fearless, and to trust in your own inner guidance.

the changing lines offer additional insights into the hexagram's message. 
line 5, for example, says "*persistently ill, and still does not die*" — this line is a warning against getting stuck in a state of stagnation, against letting your enthusiasm and energy flag. 
it's a reminder that even when things are tough, you have the power to persevere, to keep moving forward, and to trust that you'll emerge stronger and more resilient on the other side. 
this line is a gift, really — it's a reminder that you're capable of more than you think, and that you have the power to transform your challenges into opportunities.

the relating hexagram is **bringing together (#45)**, a hexagram that names a time of great success and achievement. 
this is where the energy of delight is headed — it's the destination, the culmination of all that enthusiasm and effort. 
when the changing lines complete their movement, delight becomes bringing together, and the energy shifts from one of individual enthusiasm to one of collective celebration. 
this is a hexagram that sees the value in community, in gathering people together, and in honoring the achievements of others — it's a time to come together, to share in each other's successes, and to rejoice in the beauty of human connection.
