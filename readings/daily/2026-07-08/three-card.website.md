## the thread

the underlying hum for this whole journey is one of feeling a bit out of sync, a subtle isolation that the hermit reversed brings to the table. it’s not always a choice, this withdrawal, but a feeling of being separate. this separation might have been what allowed the past’s social dynamics to sour — the three of cups reversed. when connection isn't genuine, or when you're feeling on the periphery, it's easier to slip into less direct ways of navigating things.

that leads us directly to the present’s seven of swords reversed. the isolation, the feeling of not being fully seen or understood, can often lead to self-protective maneuvers, to keeping secrets, or even to a quiet kind of self-deception. but the sky—with the moon in virgo demanding accountability and the sun in leo pushing you to stand in your own light—is making it clear that the time for hidden agendas, even subtle ones, is over. it’s a tough reveal, but it’s a necessary one. mercury in cancer means that whatever you say now will carry significant emotional weight, so choose your words carefully, but speak them honestly.
