## the thread

that initial gentle opening from the page of cups? it led you right into the moon's fog. what you started to feel, what you were curious about, has now dissolved into something less tangible, more intuitive. it’s like someone turned down the lights, and you’re navigating by moonlight — everything is shadows and potential meaning, rather than solid ground. the devil’s undertone here is key: the uncertainty you’re feeling might be directly tied to an attachment, a pattern, or a bond that’s just below the surface. you’re seeing it, or sensing it, but not quite *seeing through* it yet.

the moon in pisces amplifies this, pulling you deeper into your feeling world, making the boundaries between reality and imagination thin. it’s hard to make sense of things when your internal compass is swimming in such a vast emotional sea. add mars in taurus to that, and you’re likely feeling stuck, or moving with a deliberate slowness through this uncertainty, maybe even resisting the clarity that wants to emerge because the familiar (even if it's veiled) feels safer. and with the sun squaring neptune, your entire sense of what’s real, what’s true for *you*, is currently up for debate. it’s a time when you might idealize, or project, or simply feel a bit lost in the current.
