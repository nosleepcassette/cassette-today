## the spread
**Strength (reversed)**, _self-doubt, weakness, harshness, insecurity_, is a call to examine how you've been handling your instincts — with shame, fear, or overcontrol, the confidence that once flowed through you has been draining away. You've been questioning your ability to trust yourself, and it's led to a sense of weakness, a feeling that you're not strong enough to handle the situations that come your way. This reversal is a signal that it's time to rebuild that trust, to find a way to reconnect with your inner self without slipping into aggression or defensiveness.

## the sky
**The moon in Virgo** wants things accounted for, it's a time for attention to detail, for analyzing the situation, and making sure everything is in its right place. This energy is all about being practical, responsible, and hardworking, and it's a great time to get things organized, to tie up loose ends, and to make a plan. 
It's a very grounded feeling. 
You can feel the earth beneath your feet.

**Venus in Leo** is a call to creativity, to passion, and to self-expression, it's a time to let your hair down, to have fun, and to enjoy the beauty of life. This energy is all about living in the moment, being spontaneous, and taking risks, and it's a great time to pursue your desires, to follow your heart, and to let your spirit soar. 
It's a wild, exhilarating ride. 
You can feel the rush of adrenaline.

## the thread
When you look at these cards together, you see a story of transformation, of growth, and of finding your way. The reversed **Strength** shows you where you've been, in a place of self-doubt and weakness, but the **Two of Swords** indicates that you're currently in a state of suspension, weighing your options, and trying to make a decision. The **Queen of Wands** shows you where you're going, to a place of confidence, creativity, and warmth, and the transits, with the **moon in Virgo** and **Venus in Leo**, provide the context, the energy that's supporting this journey. 
It's a complex, multifaceted process. 
You're being called to embody your own inner queen, to find your confidence, your creativity, and your warmth, and to let that radiate out into the world. 
The **Queen of Wands** is a powerful symbol of what it means to be a strong, independent, and creative force in the world, and she's calling to you, urging you to step into your own power, to trust yourself, and to let your spirit shine. 
It's a journey, not a destination. 
You're already on your way.
