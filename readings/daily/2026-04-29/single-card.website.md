## the card

**Judgement**
*awakening, reckoning, absolution, rebirth, calling*

Judgement calls the past forward so it can be answered, integrated, and redeemed in a higher form. This card often marks awakening, life review, or the unmistakable sense that a calling can no longer be postponed.

## the sky

**Sun in Taurus** (3rd house) — very practical, often skeptical and stubborn, and loves serenity and inner peace. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with communicating to and receiving from the environment.

**Moon in Libra** (8th house) — affectionate, tolerant, often indecisive, appreciates beauty, and seeks balance, relationship, and calmness. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with sex, death, the occult, and other hard to grasp topics.

**Mercury in Aries** (2nd house) — forceful, energetic, direct, courageous, and seeks adventure and challenge. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with self image, self worth, and material security.

## what it means together

Judgement under a sky with sun in taurus (3rd house) is specific. this isn't a generic "card of the day" — it's a card responding to an actual astrological moment. the question isn't what the card means in a vacuum. it's what the card means *right now*, with this sky, in this house.
