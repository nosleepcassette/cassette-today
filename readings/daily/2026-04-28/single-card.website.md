## the card

**Six of Cups**
*nostalgia, kindness, innocence, reunion, memories*

The Six of Cups brings sweetness from the past into the present through memory, reunion, or acts of kindness. It can indicate innocence, comfort, or healing rooted in sincere emotional exchange.
