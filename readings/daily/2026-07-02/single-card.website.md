you're surrounded by the people who make life worth living — the friends who know how to celebrate the little things, the community that shows up when you need it most. the three of cups is a card of shared joy, of emotional nourishment, of gathering around the table to break bread and make memories. it's a reunion, a collaboration, a simple pleasure in good company. 
it feels like home.
the warmth of this card is undeniable — it's a sense of belonging, of being seen and heard and understood. 

## the sky
**Venus in Leo** (5th house) — the spark of creativity and romance is ignited in your heart, and you can't help but want to share it with the people you love. 
**the moon in Virgo** — the moon wants things accounted for, and your emotions are no exception: you're craving a sense of order and balance in your relationships. 
**Jupiter in Aries** — your desire for independence and adventure is at an all-time high, but that doesn't mean you can't bring your friends along for the ride.

## what it means together
when you put it all together, the three of cups is a beautiful reminder that community is key — and that doesn't just mean the people you surround yourself with, but also the way you show up for them. with venus in leo sparking your creative flame, you're inspired to bring your passions and interests to the table, to share them with the people who matter most. but the moon in virgo keeps things grounded, reminding you that it's not just about the big, flashy gestures — it's about the little things, too, the everyday moments of connection and care. and with jupiter in aries urging you to take the leap, to be bold and fearless, you're reminded that even in the midst of community and connection, you still have your own path to follow. 
it's a delicate balance, but one that's worth striving for. 
you are not alone.
