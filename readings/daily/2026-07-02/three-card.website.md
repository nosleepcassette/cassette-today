## the spread
**Five of Wands** (_conflict, competition, friction, testing_) — you've been in a space where it feels like everyone's pushing against each other, trying to prove themselves. This energy can be sharp, it can cut both ways — on one hand, it refines your skills, makes you dig deep to find what you're truly made of. On the other, it can leave you feeling bruised, like you're just trying to survive.

You're stronger than you think.

**Death (reversed)** (_resistance, stagnation, fear of endings, clinging_) — right now, you're in a place where change is happening, but you're not letting go of what's already gone. This resistance is making the transition feel longer, more painful than it needs to be.

## the sky
**Mercury in Cancer** (3rd house) — your thoughts are deeply personal right now, influenced by memories and emotions from your past. 
**Venus in Taurus** (2nd house) — your sense of self-worth is tied to what you value, what makes you feel secure and comfortable. 
**Mars in Aries** (1st house) — you're driven by a desire to act, to take the lead, to pioneer your own path.

## the thread
The Five of Wands, Death reversed, and The Moon — these cards are about the process of transformation, about how you're being shaped and reshaped by the forces around you. 
You've been through a conflict, a testing ground where you've had to find your strength and your voice. Now, you're in a place where you need to let go of what's holding you back — the fear of change, the fear of the unknown. 
The Moon ahead suggests that the future is uncertain, but also full of possibility. It's a call to trust your intuition, to listen to your inner voice, even when the external world is confusing. 
The thread between these cards is about movement, about flowing from one state to another. It's not always easy, but it's necessary. 
You're being called to evolve, to adapt, to find new ways of being in the world. 
It's scary, but it's also an opportunity. 
You get to choose how you navigate this journey. 
Take a breath. 
Let go. 
Move forward.
