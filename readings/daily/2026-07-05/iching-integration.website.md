**Increasing (#42)**
*WIND, THUNDER, THE GENTLE, increase, furthering, undertaking, crossing, imitation, fault-ridding*

the image of hexagram 42 is wind and thunder— quite literally, the powerful force of wind driving rain and thunder across the land, making everything grow. it’s not just a gentle breeze; it’s the kind of weather that nourishes the earth, ensures a good harvest. the superior man, seeing this, understands that true increase isn't just about what you gain, but how you cultivate yourself. you see what’s good in others, you imitate it. you find your own flaws, you get rid of them. it’s an active, internal process of becoming better, more fertile ground.

the judgment for this hexagram is clear: *INCREASE. It furthers one to undertake something. It furthers one to cross the great water.* this isn't passive growth, where things just happen to you. this is an instruction to engage, to lean into the effort. if there’s something you’ve been considering, some big leap, some challenging endeavor—the time is now. it's telling you that the conditions are ripe for progress, especially if that progress requires courage and movement into the unknown. don’t wait for the tide; cross the ocean.

hexagram 42 names a time of natural abundance, yes, but it’s an abundance that demands your participation. it sees you in a moment where resources, energy, or opportunities are flowing towards you, or are available to be drawn in. this isn't just financial increase—it’s an expansion of your capabilities, your wisdom, your influence. but it’s not a blank check. the posture it demands is one of active self-improvement and generous action. you’re meant to not only receive but also to contribute, to ensure the increase circulates. it’s about growing your garden, sure, but also sharing the harvest, understanding that true prosperity comes from a cycle of giving and receiving. you’re called to be the agent of this growth, both within yourself and in your environment, identifying where you can add value, where you can improve, where you can take the necessary steps, however daunting.

no changing lines were cast for this day, which means the core message of hexagram 42 remains static and central. you’re not being directed towards a new outcome or a subtle shift in the situation; instead, you’re being asked to fully inhabit and understand the current state of increase and its demands.

## relating hexagram: Increasing (#42) 益

when no changing lines are present, the hexagram you receive is also the hexagram you are moving towards. this isn't a lack of movement, but a deepening of the present lesson. it means you are not transitioning *to* a state of increase, you are already *in* it. this day is about understanding and integrating the implications of being in a period of Increase. it’s asking you to settle into this flow, to actively participate in the growth that is already underway. the destination is simply a more profound embodiment of the journey itself. you are meant to strengthen your position in this cycle of expansion, to become more adept at both receiving and facilitating increase.

## synthesis
