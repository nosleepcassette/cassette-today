then, the page of wands bursts onto the scene, translating that internal sensitivity and newfound curiosity into outward action. it’s taking those intuitive nudges from the cups and actually *doing* something with them. you’re ready to experiment, to follow the threads of passion that pull you forward. this isn't a random jump; it’s a direct response to the empress’s call for renewal, filtered through the gentle insight of the page of cups. the magician's influence here is key: you are actively, consciously, using your willpower to move from a place of being stuck to one of eager, joyful discovery. you're not waiting for permission anymore. you're creating the adventure yourself.
