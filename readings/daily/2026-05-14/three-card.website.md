## the sky

**Venus in Taurus** (2nd house) — material desire stabilizes, asking you to put real weight behind what you actually value.

**Mercury in Gemini** (3rd house) — the mind moves fast, scattering the morning's thoughts across a dozen open tabs.

**Jupiter in Cancer** (4th house) — emotional territory expands, pulling the focus inward to make the private world much larger.

## the thread

the ten of cups set the undertone this morning — harmony, family, the big emotional baseline. that is the floor you are standing on today, even if you cannot feel it through your shoes. the sky is thick with taurus and cancer right now, making everything exceptionally physical and deeply personal. venus in taurus wants things you can touch. jupiter in cancer wants a home that can hold all of your feelings. mercury in gemini is just spinning the dial, making you think you have to figure this out aloud. you do not.

the thread running through the spread is about finally dropping the white-knuckle grip. strength in your past means you already did the hardest part — you survived the intensity without becoming it. the knight of cups showing up now is the direct result of that softening. you made room inside yourself, and the universe is filling it with something that actually tastes good. let it. the nine of cups at the end is not a trick. it is the logical conclusion of deciding that your life gets to feel good. 

the morning's ten of cups promised a baseline of harmony. the spread says you are walking right into it. take the invitation. eat the meal. the other shoe is not dropping.
