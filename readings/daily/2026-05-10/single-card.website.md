## the card

**Seven of Wands** *defense, perseverance, conviction, courage, resistance*

The Seven of Wands asks you to hold your ground when pressure rises and opposition tests your position. It is a card of conviction, persistence, and refusing to surrender a hard-won advantage.

seven of wands shows up when you're the one standing on the hill and everyone else is trying to take it. not because you're wrong — because you're visible. when you take a position that matters, opposition isn't a sign you chose badly. it's a sign you chose something worth defending.

this card isn't about aggression. it's about the moment after you've already committed and the reaction starts coming in. the question isn't "should I fight?" — you're already in it. the question is "what am I actually defending here?" if it's ego, let it go. if it's something real, stand.

the advantage in the keywords is literal: you have the high ground. the seven of wands says the position you're holding is stronger than it feels right now. the exhaustion is real but the collapse isn't coming. keep your feet planted.
