## the card

**Death**
*endings, transformation, release, transition, renewal*

Death signifies a real ending that clears space for transformation, whether or not the ego welcomes it. It strips away what has completed its purpose so life can continue in a truer form.

## the sky

**Sun in Taurus** (3rd house) — practical, often skeptical and stubborn, and loves serenity and inner peace. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with communicating to and receiving from the environment.

**Moon in Libra** (8th house) — affectionate, tolerant, often indecisive, appreciates beauty, and seeks balance, relationship, and calmness. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with sex, death, the occult, and other hard to grasp topics.

**Mercury in Aries** (2nd house) — forceful, energetic, direct, courageous, and seeks adventure and challenge. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with self image, self worth, and material security.

## what it means together

Death under a sky with sun in taurus (3rd house) is specific. this isn't a generic "card of the day" — it's a card responding to an actual astrological moment. the question isn't what the card means in a vacuum. it's what the card means *right now*, with this sky, in this house.
