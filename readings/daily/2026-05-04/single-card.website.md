## the card

**Seven of Cups**
*imagination, options, fantasy, temptation, illusion*

The Seven of Cups fills the field with alluring possibilities, not all of them practical or true. It asks for discernment so desire can become a real choice instead of endless fantasy.

## the sky

**Sun in Taurus** (1st house) — practical, often skeptical and stubborn, and loves serenity and inner peace. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with establishment of personal identity.

**Moon in Sagittarius** (8th house) — jovial, open minded, loves freedom, and seeks meaning and new experience. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with sex, death, the occult, and other hard to grasp topics.

**Mercury in Taurus** (12th house) — very practical, often skeptical and stubborn, and loves serenity and inner peace. Most often this manifests in the area of life dealing with things that disrupt or cause disassociation with the personality.

## what it means together

Seven of Cups under a sky with sun in taurus (1st house) is specific. this isn't a generic "card of the day" — it's a card responding to an actual astrological moment. the question isn't what the card means in a vacuum. it's what the card means *right now*, with this sky, in this house.
