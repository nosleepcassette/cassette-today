*scattered will, aggression, lack of control, delay, directionless force*

you’re feeling a powerful surge right now, a drive to move forward — but it’s not quite landing. the chariot, upright, is all about focused will and disciplined victory, but reversed, it’s like someone handed you the keys to a tank and forgot to mention the steering wheel is missing. you have the engine, the momentum, perhaps even a destination in mind, but the actual navigation? that’s another story. it’s less about stopping, more about swerving.

there’s a lot of force at play, a clear intensity in your actions, but it might be scattered, pushing in multiple directions at once, or perhaps just spinning the wheels in place. are your efforts coordinated, or are they just... intense? sometimes, when the reins are loose, emotion takes the lead, pulling you along a path you didn't consciously choose. you might feel frustrated — aggressive, even — because you’re expending so much energy without seeing clear progress. it’s exhausting, this kind of forward motion.
