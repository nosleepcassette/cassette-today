*release, reclaiming power, breaking chains, detox, awareness*

you've been carrying something that isn't yours to carry. maybe you've known it for a while — that low hum of obligation, the relationship that costs more than it gives, the habit you keep returning to even though it leaves you empty. the devil reversed doesn't wait for you to be ready. it shows up when the lie has worn thin enough that you can see through it.

the chain around your neck? it's loose. not gone yet — but loose enough that you could slip it off if you wanted to. that's the tricky part. the devil upright makes you believe you have no choice. reversed, it shows you the door and asks if you're actually going to walk through it. the bond might still be there. the craving might still flicker. but something has shifted in your awareness, and you can't unsee it.

this is not a gentle card. it's the morning after, the hangover of a pattern you've outgrown, the quiet realization that the cage was never locked from the outside. you don't have to burn everything down today. but you do have to admit what you already know.

## the sky

**Saturn in Aries (7th house)** — the contracts you signed when you were desperate are coming up for renegotiation, and the other party doesn't get to set the terms anymore.

**Jupiter in Cancer (10th house)** — something you've been protecting is about to become visible in a way that actually helps you, not exposes you.

## what it means together

saturn in aries in the 7th is the part of you that made deals to keep the peace — partnerships, agreements, compromises that felt necessary at the time. the devil reversed mirrors that energy back: the agreement you made with your own captivity. you said you'd stay small in exchange for safety. you said you'd keep the attachment because leaving felt worse. saturn says the bill is due, and aries says you get to fight your way out.

jupiter in cancer in the 10th is where this gets interesting. the thing you've been hiding — the messy need, the shame around what you've tolerated, the part of you that's still tangled — it's not going to destroy you when it surfaces. jupiter in cancer doesn't punish vulnerability. it rewards it. the devil reversed is the first step of letting that hidden thing see light, not because you're ready, but because the containment was costing more than the exposure ever could.

you don't have to be free by tonight. you just have to stop pretending the chain is lock.
