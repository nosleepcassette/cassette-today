## the card

**Two of Wands**
*planning, future vision, personal power, choice, expansion*

The Two of Wands is the moment after the standoff resolves. Where yesterday's Two of Swords refused to look, the Two of Wands is already looking — scanning the horizon, measuring the distance, deciding how far you're willing to reach. This card is about personal power deployed toward a future you've chosen. Not wishful thinking. Strategic vision backed by the will to make it real.

The sun in taurus in the 1st house doubles down on this: you're standing in your own position, planted, and the question isn't whether you have the capacity to move. It's how far you're willing to let that reach extend.

## the sky

**Sun in Taurus** (1st house) — identity is fixed and grounded. You're not guessing who you are right now. The taurus sun in the 1st is the most stubborn version of selfhood — not rigid, but immovable when it matters. Whatever you decide to reach for, you'll reach from solid ground.

**Moon in Sagittarius** (8th house) — emotionally, the desire for expansion and meaning is still running through deep territory. The 8th house doesn't let sagittarius just fly. It says: you can have the big vision, but you have to earn it by passing through what's uncomfortable. The emotional register today is someone who wants more and knows more costs something.

**Mercury in Taurus** (12th house) — the mind is working quietly, slowly, below conscious awareness. The 12th house is where answers incubate. You might not be able to articulate the plan yet, but the plan is forming. Trust the processing even if it feels like nothing is happening upstairs.

## what it means today

The two of wands following the two of swords is a genuine shift. Yesterday was the refusal to decide. Today is the decision starting to form — not announced, not declared, but visible from where you're standing. The sagittarius moon in the 8th says the vision you're reaching toward isn't small or safe. It's something that would actually change the structure of your life. And the taurus sun says you have the ground to reach from.

Mercury in the 12th is the quiet part. Don't try to think your way to the answer today. Let it come. The two of wands isn't about executing the plan — it's about seeing it. The execution comes later.

After a week of cups and major arcana (knight of cups, six of cups, judgement, death), the shift to wands is significant. The emotional and transformative intensity of the last five days is turning into direction. Feeling is becoming intention. The question the two of wands asks: what are you willing to claim?
