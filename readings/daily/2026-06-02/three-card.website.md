*balance, moderation, healing, integration, harmony, family, belonging, peace, abundance, nurture, creativity, fertility* 
you're coming from a place of balance and moderation — temperance has been blending opposites, healing, and making things sustainable over time. this is a patient process, not a dramatic one — it's about integration, not extremes. and now, in the present, you're feeling a sense of harmony, of belonging, of peace that extends beyond a fleeting mood. the ten of cups is about enduring emotional harmony, and the joy of shared life — it's family, chosen kin, and the kind of peace that comes from being with people who get you. 
but what's interesting is that this morning, the empress showed up in a reversed position — smothering, stagnation. that set the undertone for today. 
you're looking to the future, and the empress is there, bringing fertile, embodied growth, and the ability to nourish what matters until it ripens. she's about creativity, caregiving, pleasure, or material conditions that support flourishing — but with this morning's reversal, you might be feeling a little stuck, a little smothered. 

## the sky
**Moon in Virgo** — the moon in virgo wants things accounted for, and it's not afraid to get a little nitpicky to make sure everything is just so. 
**Venus in Taurus** — venus in taurus is all about pleasure, and beauty, and the kind of comfort that comes from being surrounded by nice things. 
**Jupiter in Aries** — jupiter in aries is a bit of a wild card — it's about expansion, and exploration, and taking risks, but it can also be a little reckless, a little impulsive. 

## the thread
so what does it all mean, together? well, you've got this balance, this moderation, this healing energy from temperance, and it's led you to a place of harmony, of belonging, of peace. but there's this undertone of stagnation, of smothering, from the reversed empress — and that's making you feel a little stuck, a little uncertain about how to move forward. 
the moon in virgo wants you to get things just so, to make sure everything is accounted for — and that's a good thing, because it can help you get unstuck, get moving again. but venus in taurus is all about pleasure, and beauty, and comfort — and that's what you're really craving, deep down. you want to feel good, you want to feel comfortable, you want to be surrounded by nice things. 
and then there's jupiter in aries, which is all about expansion, and exploration, and taking risks — it's a little reckless, a little impulsive, but it's also what's going to help you break free from that stagnation, that smothering feeling. 
so what do you do? you take a deep breath, you get things accounted for, you make sure everything is just so — and then you take a risk, you explore, you expand. you let yourself feel pleasure, and beauty, and comfort — and you trust that the universe is going to support you, is going to help you flourish. 
it's not always easy, but it's what you need to do — you need to trust, you need to let go, you need to allow yourself to feel good, to feel comfortable, to be surrounded by nice things. 
you are enough.
