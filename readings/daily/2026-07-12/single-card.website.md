*missed chance, poor planning, scarcity, instability, short-term thinking*
you're looking at a chance that's not being taken — or not being taken well. the ace of pentacles reversed suggests that the opportunity is there, but it's being mishandled, delayed, or undercut by poor planning and insecurity. the seed exists, but it may not yet have the conditions needed to take root. this is a card about potential that's not being met — and that's a hard thing to swallow. 
it's a small thing, at first. 
then it's not.

## the sky
**the moon in virgo** — the moon wants things accounted for, and it's not getting that right now. 
**venus in leo** — love and value are trying to express themselves, but it's coming out a bit showy, a bit insecure. 
**mars in gemini** — action and energy are scattered, trying to find a focus.

## what it means together
the ace of pentacles reversed, with the moon in virgo, suggests that you're not just missing a chance — you're missing it because you're not paying attention to the details. you're not making a plan, or you're not sticking to it, and that's causing the instability. the venus in leo adds a layer of insecurity to this — you're not just worried about the practicalities, you're also worried about being seen, about being valued. and the mars in gemini just makes everything feel more frantic, more scattered. 
you need to take a breath, and look at the details. 
what's the plan? 
then, make it happen.
