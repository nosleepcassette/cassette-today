**Advance (#11)**
*EARTH, HEAVEN, THE RECEPTIVE, Advance*

heaven and earth unite — that's the image for peace, for advance. it’s a picture of perfect harmony, where the strong descends and the yielding ascends, meeting in the middle. it means the world is literally opening up, the highest forces connecting with the deepest foundations. think of it as the universe breathing a sigh of relief, everything aligning to support growth. your role in this isn't to force it, but to recognize it, to divide and complete your own course in accordance with this natural flow, aiding yourself and those around you by simply being present for the good that's arriving.

the judgment is clear: *“peace. the small departs, the great approaches. good fortune. success.”* this isn't a wish, it’s a statement of fact for this moment. the petty, the restrictive, the things that hold you back—they're losing their grip. what's expansive, what's truly beneficial, that's what's making its way to you. this is a time to trust that the tide is turning, that the current is carrying you towards something bigger, something more aligned with your highest good. don’t fight the departure of the small, welcome the approach of the great.

this hexagram names a time of profound shift, a period when the natural order itself is working in your favor. it asks for a posture of receptivity, a willingness to let go of what no longer serves and to open your hands—and your heart—to what is coming. it sees that the barriers are softening, the path is clearing, and the momentum is building for significant progress. this isn't about striving against the current; it's about sailing with it, recognizing the favorable winds and setting your sails accordingly. you're not meant to push uphill right now, but to move with a natural, almost effortless surge forward.

### the changing line

the first line changes today, instructing us further: "when ribbon grass is pulled up, the sod comes with it. each according to his kind. undertakings bring good fortune." this image is so grounded, so specific. you know how some plants are interconnected, their roots forming a network? pull one, and the others nearby follow. it’s a subtle but powerful reminder that your actions, especially now, aren't isolated. when you start to clear out one thing – one habit, one old belief, one relationship that's past its prime – other connected things will naturally come along for the ride. this isn’t chaos; it’s an organic, connected clearing. your undertakings now, especially those focused on gentle removal and allowing natural connections to reveal themselves, are fortunate. everything is linked, and when you move, the whole system shifts with you.

### relating hexagram: Growing Upward (#46) 升

when the movement of hexagram 11 completes its course, when the ribbon grass has been pulled and the new growth begins, you arrive at hexagram 46, growing upward. its judgment tells you: *“pushing upward has supreme success. one must see the great man. fear not. departure toward the south brings good fortune.”* this is your destination, the resolution of today's energy. you're not just advancing; you're actively pushing upward, climbing, gaining elevation. the initial peace and harmony of advance gives way to deliberate, successful growth. it’s about recognizing the leaders, the guides, the 'great man' within yourself or in your environment, who can show you the next steps. this isn't a time for timidity; it's a time to release fear and move confidently towards expansion, towards the warmth and light of the south. the arc is clear: from a natural, harmonious opening to a conscious, successful ascent.

## synthesis

the morning began with the ace of pentacles reversed, a quiet alarm. it speaks of a missed opportunity, a blocked beginning, perhaps a gift not quite received or an idea that hasn't found its footing. it’s a pause, a potential for something to slip through your fingers, or perhaps you're simply not seeing the fertile ground that's right there. this feeling of slight unreadiness or non-reception lands directly on hexagram 11’s promise that “the great approaches.” the universe is sending its gifts, its opportunities, its peace — but are you open to receiving them? the ace reversed suggests there's a subtle resistance, a hand not quite outstretched.

the future, the five of pentacles, is a stark warning. it shows a path of hardship, exclusion, feeling lost and unsupported. this is the consequence if you remain caught in the inertia of the six of cups, if you fail to open your hands to the ace of pentacles’ opportunities, if you don't engage with the hexagram's call to advance and push upward. the i ching isn't a passive prediction; it’s an active instruction. it tells you the conditions are ripe for success and upward movement. the tarot shows you where you *could* get stuck, the potential pitfalls on the journey. the devil was the past bind, the six of cups is the present comfort that could become a trap, and the five of pentacles is the future if you don't break free and take the initiative to advance.

the thread tying this all together is agency. advance is happening, the great is approaching — but you have to meet it. the ribbon grass line confirms that when you start to shift one thing, others follow. this means breaking free from the lingering binds of the devil, even if they feel familiar. it means not getting lost in the comfort of the six of cups, but using that peace as a foundation to build from, not retreat into. you are meant to be growing upward, to be pushing for supreme success, to recognize your own strength and not fear the new path. don't let the ace of pentacles reversed be a missed chance; open your hand, accept the fertile seed. the future five of pentacles is not inevitable if you embrace the energy of advance and actively choose to grow. it means taking the opportunity that is presenting itself, even if you don't immediately recognize it as "great." it's about seeing the new path, not dwelling on the old one.
