## the card

**Ten of Swords**
*ruin, endings, betrayal, collapse, exhaustion*

The Ten of Swords is a painful ending, collapse, or point of total mental exhaustion. It is stark, but it also suggests the worst has already happened and a new dawn waits on the far edge of the scene.
