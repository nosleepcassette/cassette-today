*joy, success, vitality, clarity, security, control, conservation, stability, completion, integration, fulfillment, accomplishment*

you're coming out of a period where things were unclear — The Sun is shining a light on your life, and it's bringing warmth and vitality to the areas that were previously shrouded in doubt. this is a beautiful thing, because it means you can finally see what's been going on, and you can start to make decisions based on a clear understanding of your situation. The Sun is all about being visible, and being seen — it's a card of joy and success, but it's not just about feeling good, it's about being in a place where you can be fully yourself, without fear of judgment or rejection. 
you breathe a sigh of relief. 

now, in the present moment — you're holding on tight, with the **Four of Pentacles**. this card is all about security, control, and conservation — it's about protecting what's yours, and making sure you have a sense of stability in your life. this can be a good thing, because it means you're being prudent, and you're thinking about the future — but it can also be a sign that you're afraid of loss, and you're holding on too tight. 
you feel a sense of tension.

looking ahead, **The World** is waiting for you — this card is all about completion, integration, and fulfillment. it's a card of mastery, and of being part of something larger than yourself. it's a beautiful feeling, knowing that you've accomplished something, and that you're ready to move on to the next stage of your journey. 
you feel a sense of anticipation.

## the sky
**Venus in Leo** (5th house) — the desire for creative expression and love is burning bright, and it wants to be seen. 
**Moon in Virgo** (6th house) — the need for things to be accounted for, and for life to be organized, is strong. 
**Saturn in Pisces** (12th house) — the call to dissolve boundaries, and to let go of the need for control, is whispering in the wind.

## the thread
so, how do these cards relate to each other — what's the story they're telling. well, it seems like you're coming out of a period of darkness, and into a place of light and clarity — The Sun is shining, and you can finally see what's been going on. but, in the present moment, you're holding on tight, with the **Four of Pentacles** — you're afraid of loss, and you're trying to control everything. 
this is understandable, given what you've been through — but, it's also important to remember that you can't hold on too tight, or you'll suffocate. 
the **Four of Pentacles** is a card of security, but it's also a card of fear — and, if you let that fear take over, you'll never be able to fully embrace the beauty and wonder of The World, which is waiting for you in the future. 
you need to find a balance between holding on, and letting go — between controlling, and surrendering. 
the moon in virgo wants things accounted for — it wants you to be organized, and to have a sense of structure in your life. but, saturn in pisces is calling you to dissolve those boundaries — to let go of the need for control, and to surrender to the unknown. 
this is a delicate dance, but it's one you need to master — because, if you can find a way to balance these opposing forces, you'll be able to move into the future with a sense of freedom, and joy. 
you feel a sense of hope.

the page of cups, which set the undertone for this reading, is a card of sensitivity, and intuition — it's a reminder that you need to listen to your inner voice, and to trust your instincts. 
this is especially important, given the fear and control that's present in the **Four of Pentacles** — you need to find a way to quiet that fear, and to listen to the whispers of your heart. 
you take a deep breath.
