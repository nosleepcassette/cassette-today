## the thread

this isn't a story about crisis. it's a story about a person who has been doing hard things quietly for a long time, who is now at a moment where the hard things are becoming visible — to others, possibly to herself. the thread running through today is: you have already done the work this moment requires. the king of cups didn't appear in your past by accident. you know how to feel and function at the same time. that's the skill the seven of wands is going to ask for.
